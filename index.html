power: 8

The exponent used in the fractal equation, governing the shape of the Mandelbulb.

iteration: 1

The number of times the fractal equation is applied to each vertex, getting closer and closer to the true 3-dimensional set.

vertices: 100k

The number of vertices used to approximate the fractal shape. Greater than 600k renders 1/6th of the full bulb to make extreme detail possible.
